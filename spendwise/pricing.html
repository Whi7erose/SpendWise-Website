FAQ

Common questions.


Does SpendWise need my bank password or login credentials?
+

No. SpendWise never asks for bank credentials, passwords, or third-party login access. We only request read permission for your SMS inbox specifically bank transaction messages. Your banking app and all credentials remain entirely private and are never involved in the SpendWise setup process. All major Sri Lankan banks are supported instantly.

Which Sri Lankan banks are supported by this budgeting app?
+

SpendWise supports all major Sri Lankan banks and digital wallets including Sampath Bank, Commercial Bank, BOC, HNB, People's Bank, NSB, NDB, Seylan, Citi, HSBC, and all major digital wallets: FriMi, eZ Cash, and iPay. If your bank sends SMS transaction alerts, SpendWise can track it automatically.

What happens after the 3-month free trial ends?
+

At the end of your free trial, you'll be asked to confirm your premium subscription at LKR 1,500/month. If you choose not to upgrade, you're automatically moved to the free tier basic bank SMS tracking continues at no cost. No charge ever occurs without your explicit confirmation. There's no lock-in, no cancellation fees, and no credit card is required to start.

Is my financial data private under PDPA 2022?
+

Yes. SpendWise is built from the ground up to comply with Sri Lanka's Personal Data Protection Act No. 9 of 2022 (PDPA 2022). No personal data is ever sold. Any anonymised spending data shared with retail partners requires your explicit opt-in consent and is aggregated no individual transaction data or personal identifiers are ever shared with third parties.

Can I use SpendWise to track corporate card expenses and tax reimbursements?
+

Yes. The premium plan includes full corporate card tracking via bank SMS, automatic categorisation of fuel, meals, accommodation, and business travel, and one-click Inland Revenue-ready expense reports for tax reimbursement submission to HR or Finance. For enterprise plans covering entire teams, contact us for custom pricing.

Is SpendWise a good QuickBooks alternative for Sri Lankan SMEs?
+

Yes — SpendWise is designed as a lightweight, affordable alternative to expensive accounting software like QuickBooks and SAP. At LKR 1,500/month versus LKR 15,000+/month for QuickBooks, SpendWise provides automated expense categorisation, OCR receipt scanning, one-click professional reports, and subscription management covering 90% of what an SME needs without the enterprise price tag. No per-seat fees. No accountant add-ons.
